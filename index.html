Jed Feder is a New York-based actor, drummer, guitarist, and composer. Most recently he was on tour as Kassim in Disney’s Aladdin and appeared as Uri Savir in Broadway in Chicago and TimeLine Theatre’s co-production of Oslo.

Additionally, Jed has previously worked as an actor, a musician, and often both at Arena Stage, Chicago Shakespeare Theatre, Marriott Theatre, Drury Lane Oakbrook, Chicago Children’s Theatre, Asolo Rep., and more.

Jed is a Northwestern alum, where he studied Radio/TV/film and Music Composition. He is a proud member of Actors’ Equity and the American Federation of Musicians.
