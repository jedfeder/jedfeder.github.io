Jed Feder is a New York- and Chicago-based actor, drummer, guitarist, and composer currently on tour as Kassim in Disney’s Aladdin. He is represented by Gray Talent Group Inc.

Jed has previously worked as an actor, a musician, and often both at Arena Stage, Chicago Shakespeare Theatre, Marriott Theatre, Drury Lane Oakbrook, Chicago Children’s Theatre, Asolo Rep., and more.

Jed is a Northwestern alum, where he studied Radio/TV/film and Music Composition. He is a proud member of Actors’ Equity and the American Federation of Musicians.
